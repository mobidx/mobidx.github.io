Overview

By 2050, more than 2 billion people will be over 60, straining traditional healthcare delivery. Mobile and wireless sensing — from smart speakers capturing vital signs, to radar-equipped smart glasses tracking eye movements, to an expanding ecosystem of wearables (rings, mouthguards, jewelry) — is emerging as a transformative paradigm. Combined with 6G Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) and closed-loop agentic systems that fuse sensing with intervention, this interdisciplinary space is reshaping health. MobiDx is a new forum bringing together researchers, clinicians, and practitioners to shape this future.

Topics of Interest

  • Contactless & Physiological Sensing (radar, acoustic, ISAC, vision)
  • Emerging Wearables & Smart Everyday Objects
  • Agentic & Closed-Loop Sensing and Interventions
  • Digital Twins, Foundation Models & LLMs for Biosignals
  • Clinical Translation, Equity & Deployment in the Wild

Paper Submission

MobiDx’26 invites bold, visionary ideas as research papers (up to 6 pages), challenge papers (up to 4 pages, prefixed “Challenge:”), and demos (2 pages). Submissions must use 10pt font or larger and follow ACM double-column guidelines. All accepted papers will appear in the ACM proceedings.
